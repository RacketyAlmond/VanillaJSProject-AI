Structure: Use a clear structure: greeting, body, closing.

Formal E-mail: Use polite tone, full sentences, and professional phrases.

Informal E-mail: You can use contractions, casual greetings, and emojis (if appropriate).

Language: Keep it concise and relevant. Avoid long paragraphs.

Common Mistakes: Forgetting subject line, unclear purpose, or poor tone.
